Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How accurate are astrological predictions?

Astrological accuracy depends on precise birth data and skilled interpretation. Our predictions focus on trends and timing rather than specific events, with many clients reporting 80-90% accuracy in personality insights and life pattern recognition.

What information do I need for a reading?

We need your exact birth date, time (to the minute if possible), and location. If birth time is unknown, we can work with date and location, though some aspects of the reading may be limited.

How long does a consultation last?

Standard consultations are 60 minutes, which includes chart preparation, detailed analysis, and Q&A. We also offer 30-minute focused sessions and 90-minute comprehensive readings for complex cases.

Do you offer online consultations?

Yes! We conduct consultations via video call, phone, or in-person. Online sessions are just as effective as in-person meetings, and we serve clients worldwide.
